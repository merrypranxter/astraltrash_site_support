The deck's reels only spin while the tape rolls. The counter follows real playback time. The VU LEDs listen to the actual audio when CORS allows — otherwise they fake it convincingly.

Notes rise from the edges while music plays: louder means more of them, bigger, faster. Pause and the room settles.
